WDD 231 — Individual Project Site Plan

01 Site Name

AI Chronicles

The name AI Chronicles was chosen because it conveys a sense of ongoing history and documentation — a chronicle is a factual account of events ordered by time, which perfectly matches the mission of this site: to record and present the history of artificial intelligence from its origins to the present day.

Optional domain: aichronicles.dev

02 Site Purpose

AI Chronicles is an educational, three-page website that documents the history of artificial intelligence. The site presents an interactive timeline of key milestones, profiles of the pioneers and researchers who shaped the field, and a curated list of landmark papers and their impact. Visitors can explore and filter AI history data loaded dynamically from a local JSON source.
